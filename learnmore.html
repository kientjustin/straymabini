What is Stray?

Stray is a dedicated platform serving the Municipality of Mabini, designed to help locate, assist, and protect stray endangered animals in need. Users can report sightings or seek help for a missing pet, reunite lost pets with their owners, and connect with local shelters to ensure every animal gets the care it deserves.

How It Works

  • Users report sightings and missing pets with location and details.
  • Reports are added to a database for shelters and rescuers.
  • Communities can help reunite lost pets with their owners.

Get Involved

Join the community by reporting stray endangered animals, volunteering, or helping find a missing pet. Together, we can make a difference.
