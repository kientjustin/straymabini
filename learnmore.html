What is Stray?

Stray is a platform designed to help locate and assist stray animals in need. Users can report sightings, find lost pets, and connect with local shelters.

How It Works

  • Users report sightings with location and details.
  • Reports are added to a database for shelters and rescuers.
  • Communities can help reunite lost pets with their owners.

Get Involved

Join the community by reporting stray animals, volunteering, or adopting a pet in need. Together, we can make a difference.
